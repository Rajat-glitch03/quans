Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [29-06-2026] | Last Updated: [29-06-2026]

Welcome to QUANS ("QUANS", "we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data when using our website and AI-powered services. By accessing or using QUANS, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. 1. Who We AreQUANS is an AI-powered productivity platform that provides tools such as AI assistants, social media content generation, content repurposing, writing assistance, and additional AI-powered features. This Privacy Policy applies to: • Our website • Web applications • AI tools • Customer support • Future services operated under the QUANS brand 2. Information We CollectWe collect information in several ways. 2.1 Information You ProvideWhen creating an account, contacting us, or using our services, you may provide: • Full name • Email address • Profile picture (if signing in through Google) • Login credentials (for email/password accounts) • Messages sent to customer support • Feedback and feature requests 2.2 AI Prompts and Generated ContentWhen using QUANS, you may enter: • Questions • Prompts • Business information • Documents • Writing requests • Content ideas We also store the AI-generated responses produced for your requests. Prompt history allows you to: • Review previous generations • Continue previous work • Improve your overall experience 2.3 Technical InformationWe automatically collect certain technical information including: • IP address • Browser type • Device information • Operating system • Language preferences • Time zone • Session duration • Pages visited • Feature usage • Error logs 2.4 CookiesQUANS uses cookies and similar technologies to: • Keep you signed in • Remember your preferences • Improve website performance • Maintain security • Analyze traffic • Improve user experience You can disable cookies through your browser settings, although some features may stop working properly. 3. How We Use Your InformationWe use collected information to: • Create and manage your account. • Authenticate your identity. • Deliver AI-generated responses. • Improve the quality of our AI tools. • Maintain conversation history. • Detect abuse or fraudulent activity. • Improve system security. • Respond to customer support requests. • Notify you of important product updates. • Develop new features and services. We do not sell your personal information to advertisers. 4. AI ProcessingQUANS uses third-party AI providers to generate responses. When you submit prompts, those prompts may be securely processed by AI providers solely for the purpose of generating requested content. We choose providers that maintain appropriate security standards. However, users should avoid entering: • Passwords • Credit card numbers • Banking credentials • Government identification numbers • Medical records • Highly confidential business secrets unless absolutely necessary. 5. Account RegistrationYou may create an account using: • Google Sign-In • Email and Password You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials. You agree to notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access to your account. 6. Prompt HistoryQUANS stores prompt history to improve your experience. This allows you to: • Revisit previous conversations • Continue unfinished work • Access generated content later You may be provided options in the future to delete individual conversations or clear your history. 7. Ownership of ContentYou retain ownership of: • Your prompts • Your uploaded content • AI-generated outputs created specifically for your requests QUANS does not claim ownership of content generated for your account. However, you remain responsible for reviewing all generated content before publication or commercial use. 8. Accuracy of AI ContentArtificial Intelligence may occasionally produce: • Incorrect information • Incomplete responses • Outdated information • Inaccurate suggestions Generated content should always be reviewed before relying on it for legal, financial, medical, or business decisions. 9. Data RetentionWe retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to: • Operate the service • Maintain account functionality • Comply with legal obligations • Resolve disputes • Prevent fraud • Enforce our Terms Deleted accounts may have certain information removed or anonymized, except where retention is required by law. 10. Data SecurityProtecting user data is important to us. We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Examples include: • Secure authentication • Encrypted connections (HTTPS) • Access controls • Infrastructure security • Regular monitoring Despite these measures, no internet-based system is completely secure. 11. Sharing InformationWe may share information with trusted third parties that help us operate QUANS, including: • Authentication providers • AI service providers • Cloud hosting providers • Analytics providers • Customer support platforms • Future payment processors These providers are only given the information reasonably necessary to perform their services. We do not sell your personal information. 12. AnalyticsWe may use analytics tools to understand: • Popular features • Performance issues • User behavior • Product improvements Analytics information is generally aggregated and helps improve the platform. 13. Children's PrivacyQUANS is not intended for children below the age required under applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will take reasonable steps to remove it. 14. International ProcessingDepending on our infrastructure and service providers, your information may be processed or stored outside India. By using QUANS, you acknowledge that your information may be transferred to jurisdictions with different data protection laws. We take reasonable measures to ensure appropriate protection of your information. 15. Your RightsDepending on applicable law, you may have the right to: • Access your information • Correct inaccurate information • Delete your account • Request removal of certain personal information • Withdraw consent where applicable • Contact us regarding privacy concerns Requests may be subject to identity verification. 16. Email CommunicationsWe may send emails regarding: • Account verification • Password resets • Security notifications • Product updates • Service announcements • Customer support Where applicable, you may unsubscribe from non-essential communications. 17. Third-Party LinksOur website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices or content. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any external websites they visit. 18. Future PaymentsIf paid subscriptions are introduced, payment information will be processed through trusted third-party payment providers. QUANS does not currently store complete payment card information. Additional payment-related privacy information will be added when subscriptions become available. 19. Changes to This PolicyWe may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When significant changes are made, we may notify users through: • Website notices • Email notifications • Account announcements Your continued use of QUANS after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. 20. Contact UsIf you have questions, requests, or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us through the official contact page available on the QUANS website. We will make reasonable efforts to respond to privacy-related inquiries in a timely manner. 21. Acceptance of This Privacy PolicyBy creating an account or using QUANS, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please discontinue use of the platform.
